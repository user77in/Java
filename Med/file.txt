1. Product Overview
Problem Statement

Medical bills are:

Confusing, non-transparent, and error-prone

Frequently incorrect (duplicate charges, miscoding, out-of-network errors)

Stressful for patients, who lack the expertise to audit them

Most consumers:

Do not understand CPT/ICD codes

Do not know their rights

Do not have time to call providers

As a result, people overpay thousands of dollars unnecessarily.

Solution

A self-serve B2C SaaS that:

Accepts medical bills (PDF/photo)

Automatically audits them for common billing errors

Explains issues in plain English

Generates ready-to-send dispute letters

Tracks resolution status

5.3 Error Detection Engine (CORE VALUE)

The system flags potential issues, not definitive claims.

Error Categories

Duplicate Charges

Same CPT + same date + same provider

Upcoding

CPT code inconsistent with visit type (e.g. high-level ER for routine visit)

Unbundling

Procedures billed separately that should be bundled

Out-of-Network Misbilling

Provider listed as out-of-network when facility is in-network

Balance Billing (Illegal in many cases)

Charges beyond allowed amounts

Missing Insurance Adjustments

Insurance paid nothing when expected

Excessive Pricing

Charges far above regional averages
